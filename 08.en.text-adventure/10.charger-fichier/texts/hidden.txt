You crouch behind the largest wardrobe in the laboratory.
Despite the thickness of the wood, the wizard's spell shakes
you as it crashes against the massive piece of furniture.
You hear him approaching behind the wardrobe, chanting.
He is preparing a new spell!
Panicked, you look around. Your eyes are drawn to the glint
of a dagger set with precious stones, lying among a pile
of jewelry. You also spot the laboratory's exit door.
Finally, an idea forms in your mind.

D - Go pick up the dagger
E - Dash towards the exit
P - Push the wardrobe onto the wizard
